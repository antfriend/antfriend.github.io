The notion of 'motive' signifies the underlying reasons behind an individual's actions, serving as the starting point of their behavior. In contrast, 'motivation' encompasses the amalgam of factors that propel a person towards a certain goal, essentially dictating the trajectory of their journey and their current state of being.
